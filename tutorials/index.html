Dark Matter

Dark Matter Freeze-out and Relic Density

Compute the relic density of dark matter by numerically solving the Boltzmann equation. Covers the freeze-out mechanism, thermally-averaged annihilation cross sections, and how weak-scale interactions give rise to the observed dark matter abundance (Ωχh² ≈ 0.12). Includes exercises on the improved yield-based Boltzmann equation, relic density as a function of WIMP mass, and freeze-in as an alternative production mechanism.
